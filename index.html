TuneFlow

TuneFlow is where you can listen to music while playing your game or just listen to music.It is appropriate for ages 10 and up.There are also appropriate songs for kids. TuneFlow is also affordable. You can create a playist, send the playlist to someone, or you can even create the playlist with someone else. You can create your own music and let others give you feedback on the music you created. Every week of the day TuneFlow will show you the music you listen to the most. Every year it will show the music you listen to the most, the artist you listen to the most, the new music you have listen through out the year. TuneFlow can even recommend you music or artist based on the survey you take. They can even find you new artists and songs that you might like based on the things you listen to or the music you create. TuneFlow also has audiobooks you can listen too or podcast you can listen too. You cand even make yout own podcast and talk about stuff you wanna talk about.
